1. Description of Service

Change4Change monitors your linked bank account transactions, calculates a round-up amount (the difference between each transaction total and the next whole dollar), accumulates those amounts, and transfers the total to a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charity you select. We collect a 10% platform fee to cover operating costs.

2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and a U.S. resident with a U.S. bank account to use this Service.

3. Bank Account Connection

You authorize us to connect to your bank account via Plaid in read-only mode to retrieve transaction data. You also authorize ACH debits from your connected account for the purpose of processing your charitable donations. You may disconnect your bank account at any time.

4. Donations

  • Donations are initiated manually — we do not automatically debit your account on a fixed schedule without your confirmation.
  • All donations are final. We cannot reverse a completed ACH transfer to a charity.
  • Change4Change is not itself a nonprofit. We facilitate donations to third-party charities and do not provide tax receipts. Contact your chosen charity for donation records.

5. Platform Fee

A platform fee of 10% is deducted from each donation batch before the remainder is transferred to your chosen charity. This fee covers payment processing, infrastructure, and operational costs.
