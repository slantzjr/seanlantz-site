Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Change4Change ("we", "us", or "our") operates a charitable giving platform that rounds up your everyday purchases and donates the difference to a charity of your choice. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name and email address when you sign up.
  • Bank account data — read-only access to your transaction history and account details via Plaid. We never see or store your bank login credentials.
  • Payment information — bank account details for ACH transfers, processed by Stripe. We do not store full account numbers on our servers.
  • Charitable giving preferences — the charity you select and your donation history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate round-up amounts from your transactions.
  • To initiate ACH transfers from your bank account to your chosen charity.
  • To provide you with a record of your donations.
  • To communicate with you about your account and donations.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to provide our service:

  • Plaid Technologies, Inc. — connects to your bank account in read-only mode to retrieve transaction data. Plaid's privacy policy is available at plaid.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Stripe, Inc. — processes ACH bank transfers. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Clerk — manages user authentication and identity. Clerk's privacy policy is available at clerk.com/privacy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Bank credentials are never stored by Change4Change — they are handled exclusively by Plaid's encrypted infrastructure. Payment data is handled by Stripe's PCI-compliant systems.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Disconnect your bank account at any time through your account settings or directly via Plaid.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at sean@seanlantz.com.
