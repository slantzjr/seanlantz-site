Round up your purchases.
Give the change to charity.

Change4Change connects to your bank account, rounds up every transaction to the nearest dollar, and donates the spare change to the charitable cause you choose — automatically.

How It Works

1

Connect Your Bank

Securely link your checking account using bank-level encryption via Plaid.

2

Choose a Charity

Pick from a curated list of verified 501(c)(3) nonprofits you care about.

3

Spend Normally

Every purchase is rounded up to the nearest dollar. The difference accumulates.

4

We Donate

Your rounded-up change is transferred to your chosen charity on your behalf.

Your Data, Handled Responsibly

Change4Change uses Plaid to read your transaction history in read-only mode. We never store your bank credentials and never move money without your explicit authorization.

Donations are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We collect a small platform fee (10%) to cover operating costs; the remainder goes directly to your chosen charity.

For full details on what we collect and why, see our Privacy Policy.
